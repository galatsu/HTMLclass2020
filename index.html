I really like these movies because they've either got great visuals, or plot, or they really resemble their original material, which can be a video game (in the case of Tomb Raider) or a comic book (such as in the case of Valerian and Alita). They're all great movies, but out of the entire list, the Ghibli movies have a special place in my heart.

The reason for that is that Ghibli movies are so beautifully made overall. Howl's Moving Castle especially, since I love the whole idea of demons and magic and other otherworldly things, with the prospect of genuine love between the two, rather different main characters. I could watch that movie again and again without getting bored, and same thing applies to the other Ghibli movie examples listed.
